### 🔨 What I'm building

I'm focused on the **engineering side of ML**: making models reliable, observable
and easy to deploy. Coming from signal processing, I bring a "what does this
actually do at the input/output level" mindset to ML systems.

- **Model serving infrastructure** — FastAPI services with batching, monitoring, structured logging
- **End-to-end ML pipelines** — from raw data to deployed inference, containerised
- **MLOps fundamentals** — MLflow tracking, model registry, CI/CD for models
- **Audio + industrial ML** — leveraging my DSP background for anomaly detection, signal classification
- **Apache Airflow** — orchestrating training and batch inference workflows
